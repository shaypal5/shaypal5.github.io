**Work**: In April 2026, I started as Head of AI & Data at the Adanim Institute, an applied research institute established by the Schusterman Family Foundation and dedicated to advancing social welfare systems through systemic infrastructure, policy research, and data-driven operational methods. Alongside that work, I continue to advise organizations on data science strategy, AI implementation, team building, and applied ML systems.

Before Adanim, I worked as an independent strategic data science consultant, helping startups build and mature data science operations across technical, methodological, and organizational questions. I previously served as VP Data Science at [LeO, an insurtech startup](https://www.meetleo.com/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, and led data science work at [Neura](https://www.theneura.com/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} and [ZenCity](https://zencity.io/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}. Earlier, I worked as a developer at Intel and [Ravello](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ravello-systems/about/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} (now Oracle Cloud).

**Community Projects**: I co-founded and help lead [Datahack](http://www.datahack.org.il/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, a nonprofit promoting AI and data science in Israel through programs such as [DataCoach](https://www.datacoach.org.il/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, [DataNights](https://datanights-il.github.io/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, [DataTalks](https://www.meetup.com/DataHack){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, and Kaggle-IL. I also founded [NLPH](https://www.facebook.com/groups/157877988136954/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, a community for Hebrew NLP resources and collaboration. We have run two cohorts of the DataNights program for data science team leads, the second three years ago, and a third cohort is planned soon. See more at my [community](/community.html) page.
